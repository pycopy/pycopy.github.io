What's in the name?

Pycopy's name started as mundane "Pico Python" and went thru the following magic transmogrifications:

Pico Python → PicoPy → PycoPy → Pycopy

Rendered like this, some people want to see in the name something more than the original "Pico Python". They see distant lands and exotic cities something like "Copy of Python, with baggage removed". If you are among these people, congratulations - you have a good imagination!

Pycopy is pronounced like "pico pie", though among the Pycopy community, there's a growing faction of those who pronounce it like "pie-co-pie".
